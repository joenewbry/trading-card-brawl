# Druid Class (Life)

## **Sproutling**
- **Mana Cost:** 1 Life
- **Attack/Health:** 1/1
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Battlecry: Gain +1 Health for each other friendly creature.
- **Flavor Text:** "Even the smallest seed knows when to grow."
- **Design Notes:** Encourages token synergy and board presence. Scales with other cards in hand, rewarding deck density and creature count. Provides early defense without requiring direct removal.

## **Healing Bloom**
- **Mana Cost:** 2 Life
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Restore 3 Health to all friendly characters.
- **Flavor Text:** "The earth remembers every wound — and heals it in time."
- **Design Notes:** Reliable AoE healing. Enables long games and counters poison/disease. Synergizes with life-gain and token strategies. Low-cost, high-impact sustain.

## **Ancient Treant**
- **Mana Cost:** 6 Life
- **Attack/Health:** 5/8
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Taunt, Lifesteal, Enrage: Gain +2 Attack when damaged.
- **Flavor Text:** "It has stood since the first rain. It will stand long after the last fire."
- **Design Notes:** A resilient late-game anchor. Enrage turns incoming damage into offense, creating a feedback loop that punishes aggressive plays. Taunt and lifesteal make it a wall that outlasts most threats.

## **Rampant Growth**
- **Mana Cost:** 3 Life
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Abilities:** Gain 2 Mana Crystals this turn only. (Max 10.)
- **Flavor Text:** "The forest does not rush — it grows, and the world makes room for it."
- **Design Notes:** The class's mana acceleration engine. Allows explosive mid-game plays and enables big creatures to come down earlier than expected. Key to overcoming the class’s slow-start weakness.

## **World Tree’s Embrace**
- **Mana Cost:** 8 Life
- **Type:** Enchantment
- **Rarity:** Mythic
- **Abilities:** At the start of your turn, gain a random Life card from your deck. All friendly creatures gain +2/+2 and Immune to Poison.
- **Flavor Text:** "The roots hold the world. The leaves breathe life. The heart beats on."
- **Design Notes:** The ultimate win condition. Provides card draw, massive stat buffs, and complete immunity to the class’s main weakness (poison/disease). Rewards deck building around Life cards and creates a snowball effect that’s nearly unstoppable once activated.
