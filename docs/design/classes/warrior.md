# Warrior Class (Steel)

## Ironveil Sentinel
- **Mana:** Steel 3
- **Attack/Health:** 3/5
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Taunt, Divine Shield
- **Flavor Text:** "They don't break armor. They become it."
- **Design Notes:** The classic frontline tank. Divine Shield gives early survivability; Taunt ensures the Warrior controls the board. Low cost makes it a reliable early-game anchor. Designed to reward players for committing to a defensive posture.

## Shieldbreaker Maul
- **Mana:** Steel 4
- **Attack/Health:** 5/4
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Abilities:** Battlecry: Destroy an enemy minion with 3 or less Attack
- **Flavor Text:** "It doesn't matter how many shields you have—I've seen them all crack."
- **Design Notes:** Answers aggressive decks by removing their early threats. Encourages aggressive play from opponents, which the Warrior can then punish. High attack for cost rewards tempo play.

## Aegis of the Wall
- **Mana:** Steel 5
- **Type:** Enchantment
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Whenever you take damage, gain a 2/2 Shield with Taunt
- **Flavor Text:** "The wall doesn't stop the storm—it becomes the storm's prison."
- **Design Notes:** Turns the Warrior's weakness (taking damage) into strength. Creates a snowball effect: the more you're attacked, the more you control the board. Rewards defensive play without requiring direct removal.

## Retributive Chain
- **Mana:** Steel 6
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Deal 4 damage to an enemy. If it dies, deal 4 damage to all other enemies.
- **Flavor Text:** "One strike. One death. The rest? Just collateral."
- **Design Notes:** High-risk, high-reward removal. Rewards precise targeting and combo potential with board clears. Encourages strategic play over brute force. Synergizes with low-health taunt minions.

## The Iron Oath
- **Mana:** Steel 7
- **Attack/Health:** 6/8
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Mythic
- **Abilities:** Taunt, Enrage: Gain +2/+2 and Lifesteal
- **Flavor Text:** "They said I was dead. I was just waiting for them to get close enough to hate me."
- **Design Notes:** The ultimate Warrior payoff. Requires damage to activate, reinforcing the class's identity. Lifesteal provides resilience against sustained pressure. Slow to deploy, but impossible to ignore once active.
