# Ranger Class (Wind)

## Dustfoot Stalker
- **Mana:** Wind 1
- **Attack/Health:** 1/2
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Stealth
- **Flavor Text:** "They say the wind doesn't leave footprints—until it's time to strike."
- **Design Notes:** The classic early-game stealth threat. Low cost enables aggressive starts. Forces opponents to waste removal or ignore the threat. Core to the Ranger's hit-and-run identity.

## Galewing Trap
- **Mana:** Wind 2
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Abilities:** Set a trap. When an enemy attacks a non-stealth minion, it gets frozen and takes 3 damage.
- **Flavor Text:** "You think you're hunting? You just walked into my windstorm."
- **Design Notes:** Rewards prediction and positioning. Counters aggressive decks that rely on non-stealth attackers. Creates tempo swings without requiring direct board clears. Encourages the Ranger to control the flow of combat.

## Skydancer Blade
- **Mana:** Wind 4
- **Attack/Health:** 3/4
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Windfury, Stealth
- **Flavor Text:** "A blade that moves faster than the eye can follow is the only thing the wind fears."
- **Design Notes:** High-impact tempo play. Windfury enables lethal combos in a single turn. Stealth makes it hard to remove. Perfect for closing out games or breaking through defensive lines.

## Arrowstorm Volley
- **Mana:** Wind 6
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Deal 1 damage to all enemy minions. If any die, deal 3 more damage to the enemy hero.
- **Flavor Text:** "One arrow misses. A hundred arrows... become the sky."
- **Design Notes:** Efficient board clear with bonus hero damage. Rewards players for maintaining pressure and killing minions. Encourages aggressive minion placement from opponents. Synergizes with low-health minions as bait.

## Whisperwind, the Unseen Path
- **Mana:** Wind 7
- **Attack/Health:** 4/6
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Mythic
- **Abilities:** Stealth, Combo: Gain +4/+4 and Windfury
- **Flavor Text:** "They never saw me coming. They didn’t even know I was gone."
- **Design Notes:** The Ranger's ultimate combo finisher. Requires playing another card first, rewarding deck synergy and planning. Massive burst potential when comboed. Represents the elusive, untouchable assassin who strikes from nowhere and vanishes again.
