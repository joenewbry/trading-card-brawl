# Engineer Class (Earth)

## Card Set

### 1. Gearcore Battery
- **Mana**: 1 Earth
- **Type**: Artifact
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Abilities**: Overload
- **Effect**: At the start of your turn, gain 1 mana. Next turn, you cannot spend mana.
- **Flavor Text**: "It hums with potential—and the cost of that potential is silence."
- **Design Notes**: Core artifact engine. Provides steady mana ramp at the cost of temporary lockdown. Encourages planning and forward-thinking. Enables future Overload combos.

### 2. Turret Bot
- **Mana**: 3 Earth
- **Type**: Creature (2/3)
- **Rarity**: Uncommon
- **Abilities**: Overload, Charge
- **Effect**: Can attack immediately. Next turn, you cannot cast spells.
- **Flavor Text**: "It doesn’t think. It doesn’t feel. It just fires."
- **Design Notes**: Early aggression tool. Gives Engineer immediate board pressure despite being a slow class. Overload locks spell casting, reinforcing the trade-off: speed now, flexibility later.

### 3. Repair Drone
- **Mana**: 2 Earth
- **Type**: Creature (1/2)
- **Rarity**: Rare
- **Abilities**: Battlecry, Overload
- **Effect**: Restore 2 health to a friendly artifact. Next turn, gain 1 less mana.
- **Flavor Text**: "It fixes what’s broken… but never asks what’s next."
- **Design Notes**: Artifact sustainer. Keeps key artifacts alive, reinforcing the class’s resilience theme. Overload penalty is mild, encouraging synergistic chains (e.g., Battery → Drone → Turret).

### 4. Clockwork Colossus
- **Mana**: 6 Earth
- **Type**: Creature (5/6)
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Abilities**: Taunt, Overload
- **Effect**: At the start of your turn, gain 2 attack. Next turn, you cannot play cards.
- **Flavor Text**: "It walks with the weight of ten gears and the silence of ten engines."
- **Design Notes**: Ultimate late-game threat. Strong body, growing power, and taunt make it a win condition. The heavy Overload forces a hard pivot—engineers must play around it for multiple turns, turning their own momentum into a slow-motion avalanche.

### 5. Anvil Forge
- **Mana**: 4 Earth
- **Type**: Artifact
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Abilities**: Overload
- **Effect**: At the start of your turn, create a 1/1 Gearling. Next turn, all your artifacts gain +2 Attack.
- **Flavor Text**: "It doesn’t build machines. It builds inevitability."
- **Design Notes**: Engineered inevitability. Generates minions and buffs artifacts, creating exponential growth. Overload locks future plays, forcing the player to commit fully. Represents the Engineer’s philosophy: sacrifice now to dominate later.

---

> *Signature Mechanic: Overload* — Cards that grant extra power or income but lock your mana next turn.

> *Class Identity*: Engineer is a slow, resilient builder. It doesn’t win with speed—it wins by outlasting. Its weakness is vulnerability to early pressure and disruption. Once its artifacts are established, few can stop its grinding advance. The Overload mechanic forces strategic timing: when to push, when to wait, and when to accept the silence that follows the storm.
