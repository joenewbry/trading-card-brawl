# Necromancer Class (Shadow)

## **Bone Revenant**
- **Mana Cost:** 3 Shadow
- **Attack/Health:** 2/4
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Deathrattle: Summon a 1/1 Skeleton
- **Flavor Text:** "They don't rise because they want to — they rise because you ordered it."
- **Design Notes:** The foundational deathrattle engine. Enables early board presence without requiring mana investment. Skeletons are weak but numerous, forcing opponents to spend removal on low-value targets, setting up late-game combos.

## **Soul Drain**
- **Mana Cost:** 2 Shadow
- **Type:** Spell
- **Rarity:** Common
- **Abilities:** Deal 2 damage to a creature. Gain 2 life.
- **Flavor Text:** "Every breath taken from the fallen feeds the grave's hunger."
- **Design Notes:** Efficient life gain + removal. Rewards consistent pressure. Enables survival against rush decks and creates tempo swings by turning damage into healing.

## **Carrion Wurm**
- **Mana Cost:** 5 Shadow
- **Attack/Health:** 4/6
- **Type:** Creature
- **Rarity:** Uncommon
- **Abilities:** Lifesteal, Taunt
- **Flavor Text:** "It doesn't eat the dead — it becomes them."
- **Design Notes:** A late-game wall that punishes aggressive decks. Lifesteal synergizes with any damage-dealing mechanic, turning offense into sustain. Taunt forces engagement, buying time for combos.

## **Grave Pact**
- **Mana Cost:** 4 Shadow
- **Type:** Enchantment
- **Rarity:** Rare
- **Abilities:** Whenever a creature dies, each player loses 1 life and you gain 1 life.
- **Flavor Text:** "The graveyard is not an end — it is a ledger, and you are the accountant."
- **Design Notes:** Rewards board clears and mass removal. Creates a slow, grinding attrition win condition. Forces opponents to avoid trading creatures — a powerful psychological tool.

## **Lich King's Ascension**
- **Mana Cost:** 7 Shadow
- **Type:** Artifact
- **Rarity:** Mythic
- **Abilities:** When played, sacrifice all creatures you control. Then, return all creatures that died this game to the battlefield under your control. They gain Undying (can't be destroyed by damage).
- **Flavor Text:** "Death is not the final chapter. It is the prologue to your reign."
- **Design Notes:** The ultimate payoff card. Transforms the entire game state. Rewards careful resource management and sacrifice plays. Enables impossible board states by combining low-cost minions with high-impact resurrection. The core of the class's late-game identity.
