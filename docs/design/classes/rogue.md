# Rogue Class (Wind + Shadow)

## Card Set

### 1. Shadow Dagger
- **Mana**: 1 Shadow + 1 Wind
- **Type**: Spell
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Abilities**: Combo
- **Effect**: Deal 2 damage. If you played another card this turn, deal 2 additional damage.
- **Flavor Text**: "A blade only kills when unseen."
- **Design Notes**: Low-cost combo enabler that rewards playing multiple cards per turn. Encourages aggressive tempo and hand manipulation. Core to Rogue's burst potential.

### 2. Ghoststep
- **Mana**: 2 Shadow
- **Type**: Spell
- **Rarity**: Uncommon
- **Abilities**: Stealth
- **Effect**: Summon a 0/1 Phantom with Stealth. If you played another card this turn, it gains +2 Attack.
- **Flavor Text**: "They say the wind forgets the thief. They're wrong—it remembers every breath."
- **Design Notes**: Creates a fragile but evasive threat that scales with combo chains. Forces opponent to answer or lose board control. Reinforces the "all-in" playstyle.

### 3. Deceptive Draw
- **Mana**: 1 Wind
- **Type**: Spell
- **Rarity**: Rare
- **Abilities**: Combo
- **Effect**: Draw a card. If you played another card this turn, draw two more and discard one.
- **Flavor Text**: "You don't need more cards—you need the right ones."
- **Design Notes**: Card manipulation engine. Lets Rogue cycle through its combo pieces efficiently. Risk/reward: you gain card advantage but might discard a key piece. Critical for chain consistency.

### 4. Whispering Knife
- **Mana**: 3 Shadow + 2 Wind
- **Type**: Creature (2/4)
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Abilities**: Stealth, Combo, Deathrattle
- **Effect**: When this dies, destroy a random enemy card in hand. If you played another card this turn, destroy two.
- **Flavor Text**: "The last thing your opponent sees is the glint… then nothing."
- **Design Notes**: High-impact combo finisher. Disrupts opponent's hand and rewards chaining. The deathrattle makes it a threat even if removed. Represents the Rogue's ultimate betrayal.

### 5. Mirage Gambit
- **Mana**: 4 Shadow + 3 Wind
- **Type**: Spell
- **Rarity**: Mythic
- **Abilities**: Combo
- **Effect**: Deal 4 damage to all enemy minions. If you played another card this turn, also deal 4 damage to the enemy hero and shuffle two 1/1 Phantoms into your deck.
- **Flavor Text**: "The illusion isn't in the attack—it's in the timing."
- **Design Notes**: High-risk, high-reward board clear + burst combo finisher. Only viable after establishing tempo. Reinforces the "all-or-nothing" identity of Rogue. Win condition when combo is online.

---

> *Signature Mechanic: Combo* — Cards that gain bonus effects if another card was played this turn.

> *Class Identity*: Rogue is a fragile combo engine. It doesn’t control the board—it waits, manipulates, and strikes with overwhelming force when the opportunity is perfect. Its weakness is its fragility: if the combo doesn't connect, it dies. There is no safety net.
